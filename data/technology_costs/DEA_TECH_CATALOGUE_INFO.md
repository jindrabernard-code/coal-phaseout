# Danish Energy Agency (DEA) Technology Catalogue

## Download
The DEA Technology Catalogue is freely available at:
https://ens.dk/en/our-services/projections-and-models/technology-data

Download: **Technology data for generation of electricity and district heating**
(~7 MB Excel file)
