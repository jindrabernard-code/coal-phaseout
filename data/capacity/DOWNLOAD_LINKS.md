# ČEPS and Policy Documents — Manual Download

The following documents are relevant for the coal phase-out model but require manual download (PDFs / protected Excel files):

## ČEPS Ten-Year Network Development Plan (TYNDP)
- Latest plan: https://www.ceps.cz/en/ten-year-development-plan
- Key data: scenario assumptions, demand projections, planned new capacity

## ČEPS Annual Reports (resource adequacy data)
- https://www.ceps.cz/en/publications

## Czech National Energy and Climate Plan (NECP)
- English summary: https://www.mpo.cz/en/energy/strategic-and-conceptual-documents/national-energy-and-climate-plan/
- Direct PDF: https://ec.europa.eu/energy/sites/ener/files/documents/cz_final_necp_main_en.pdf

## Czech Coal Commission Recommendations
- https://uhelna.unikatorku.cz/en/

## ERÚ (Energy Regulatory Office) Annual Reports
- https://www.eru.cz/en/annual-reports
- Contains: installed capacity by fuel, generation, import/export statistics

## EU ETS Auction Results (EEX)
- https://www.eex.com/en/market-data/environmental-markets/eua-primary-auction-spot-download
